Global Layoffs
Project Overview

Business Problem Statement and Significance:

In the current economic climate, understanding the trends and impacts of layoffs across various sectors is critical for businesses, policymakers, and job seekers. This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of global layoffs, helping stakeholders identify patterns, hotspots, and industries most affected by layoffs. It serves as a tool for strategic planning, risk management, and proactive measures to mitigate the impact of layoffs.

Objective:

The objective of this dashboard is to offer a detailed, interactive visualization of global layoffs data from March 2020 to May 2024. It highlights key metrics such as the number of employees laid off, total companies and industries affected, and provides a breakdown by top countries, industries, companies, and stages.

Specific Aims:

  • Comprehensive Overview: Present a clear and concise overview of global layoffs, including key metrics and trends over time.
  • Geographical Insights: Highlight the top countries affected by layoffs, allowing for regional analysis and strategic planning.
  • Industry Impact: Identify the top industries impacted by layoffs, facilitating sector-specific risk assessments and interventions.
  • Company Analysis: Provide insights into the companies with the highest layoffs, aiding in competitor analysis and market understanding.
  • Stage Analysis: Examine layoffs by company stages to understand where layoffs are most prevalent, whether post-IPO, acquired, or at different funding stages.
